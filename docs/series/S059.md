# S059: Actual vs Warranted Stock Price

**Chapter 10: Competition, Finance, and Interest Rates** | **Figures**: Fig10.13 | **Period**: 1871–2011

## What This Measures

Actual real price = Shiller nominal price / BEA deflator. Shiller P* = present value of real dividends at discount rate r=7.6%. Classical warranted price from equation 10.31 in DATAintropprice sheet. Compares actual vs theoretically warranted prices to test for speculative bubbles.

Chapter 10 examines the relationship between finance, interest rates, and the real economy. Shaikh argues that the interest rate is not determined by supply and demand for loanable funds but by the competitive equalization between financial and industrial rates of return.

## From the Book

> Figure 10.13: Actual vs Warranted Stock Price
- **Actual real stock price**: Shiller nominal price deflated by BEA gross investment deflator
- **Shiller's P***: "rational" stock price = PV of real dividends at constant discount rate r=7.6%, converted from CPI-based to BEA investment deflator basis
- **Classical warranted price**: from equation 10.
