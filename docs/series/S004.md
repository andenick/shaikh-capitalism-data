# S004: Ayres Business Cycle Index

**Chapter 2: Turbulent Trends and Hidden Structures** | **Figures**: Fig2.4A, Fig2.4B, Fig2.4C | **Period**: 1831–1939

## What This Measures

Historical business cycle index compiled by Leonard P. Ayres (1939) for the Cleveland Trust Company. Monthly data (~12 observations per year) covering 1831-1939. The index is a composite indicator of economic activity with no direct modern equivalent—it predates NBER business cycle dates and modern GDP-based measures. Used to illustrate pre-1940 business cycle patterns.
