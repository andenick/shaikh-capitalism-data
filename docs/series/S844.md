# S844: Stigler Profit Rates on Assets

**Chapter 8: Debates on Concentration and Profitability** | **Figures**: Fig8.5 | **Period**: 1939–1957

## What This Measures

Load Stigler profit rates from L41 Write panel data output

Chapter 8 critiques the debate between perfect and imperfect competition theories. Shaikh shows empirically that industry concentration (measured by CR4 or CR8 ratios) does not systematically predict profitability — undermining the core claim of the structure-conduct-performance paradigm.
