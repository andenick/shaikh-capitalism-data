# S013: Final Profit Rate Measures

**Chapter 6: Capital and Profit** | **Figures**: Fig6.2, Fig6.3, Fig6.6, Fig6.7 | **Period**: 1946–2024

## From the Book

> Final profit rate measures aggregate table. Upstream input for S027, S028, S105. Contains corrected corporate NOS, capital stock, inventories, and derived profit rates.
>
> — Shaikh (2016), Appendix 6.8.II.7


## Construction

| Step | Operation | Description |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | `load` | Load upstream tables S208 (profit) and S214 (capital stock) |
| 2 | `calculate` | Corrected measures: VA, NOS, profit share, profit rate (with imputed interest + GPIM) |
| 3 | `calculate` | Conventional measures: NIPA profit rate (without corrections) |
| 4 | `calculate` | Component ratios x1, x2, x3 decomposing corrected/conventional difference |
| 5 | `calculate` | Capacity utilization and normal profit rates |
| 6 | `calculate` | Incremental rates of profit (nominal and real) |
