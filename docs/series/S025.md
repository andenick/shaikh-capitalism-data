# S025: US WPI in Gold and US Gold Price

**Chapter 5: The General Price Level** | **Figures**: Fig5.6 | **Period**: 1800–2010

## What This Measures

Extract USPPIGold (S010H) and USGoldprice (S010AA) from S010

Chapter 5 examines the general price level and the relationship between gold, money, and commodity prices. Shaikh argues that classical competition theory explains relative price movements through labor-time ratios, and that the general price level is governed by the money-commodity relationship, not by the quantity of money in circulation.
