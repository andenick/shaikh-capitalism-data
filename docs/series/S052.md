# S052: Interest Rate and Price Level

**Chapter 10: Competition, Finance, and Interest Rates** | **Figures**: Fig10.6 | **Period**: 1857–2011

## What This Measures

Long bond yield spliced from Macaulay railroad bonds (1857-1937) and Fed HS-39 corporate Aaa (1936-2010), using 1919-1937 overlap ratio for splice. PPI from Jastram (1857-1976) extended with BLS growth rates to 2011. Creates long-run nominal interest rate and price level series.

## Construction

1. Load S042
2. Splice railroad + corp Aaa bonds using 1919-1937 ratio
3. Build PPI from Jastram + BLS

## Data Sources

| Subseries | Source | Period | Units |

| S052-A | Macaulay 1938 + Fed HS-39 + ERP, spliced | 1857–2011 | Percent |
| S052-B | Jastram 1977 + BLS updates | 1857–2011 | Index |

## Extension

- **API**: FRED `AAA,PPIACO`
- **Method**: growth_rate splice at 2011
- **Continuity**: AAA = Moody Aaa corporate bond yield (same concept). PPIACO = PPI All Commodities (same as BLS WPS00000000).
- **Source**: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/AAA
