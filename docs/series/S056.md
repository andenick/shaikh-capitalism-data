# S056: Bond and Equity Returns

**Chapter 10: Competition, Finance, and Interest Rates** | **Figures**: Fig10.10 | **Period**: 1926–2010

## What This Measures

Ibbotson (2004) Table 2-2 returns: rslarge (large stocks), rbcorplt (corporate bonds long-term), rbgovlt (government bonds long-term). Updated to 2010 by David Stubbs. Annual total returns for asset classes. Used to compare equity vs bond performance over long horizon.

Chapter 10 examines the relationship between finance, interest rates, and the real economy. Shaikh argues that the interest rate is not determined by supply and demand for loanable funds but by the competitive equalization between financial and industrial rates of return.
