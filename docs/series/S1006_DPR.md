## 1. Definition

**S1006** is the Ibbotson SBBI 2004 Yearbook (table 2-2) panel of annual nominal total returns:

- Large Company Stocks (S&P 500 since 1957; S&P 90 1926–1956) — `rslarge`
- Long-Term Corporate Bonds (~20yr AAA/AA index) — `rbcorplt`
- Long-Term Government Bonds (~20yr UST) — `rbgovlt`
- 3-month Treasury Bills — `rbtbills` (also captured)
- Inflation — `inflrate` (captured)

## 2. Why it matters

Shaikh's Figure 10.6 sets up the cross-asset return comparison that motivates the corporate-IROP vs equity-return analysis in S1007.

## 7. Caveats

1. **Ibbotson licensing.** SBBI is now a Morningstar commercial product. Per decision 0005 and Phase 4 ratification, Damodaran NYU is the open modern substitute. The local `Appendix10_Ibbotson.xlsx` workbook is the authoritative academic reference for the 1926–2010 reproduction.
2. **LT Corporate Bond reconstruction is a PROXY.** Damodaran does not publish a long-corporate total-return series. The extension reconstructs an approximate total return from FRED `AAA` yield (treating the yield as a rough proxy for the running yield component of a duration-stable LT corporate index). This is flagged `proxy: true` with a Concept Match Justification in the EPR.
3. **2004–2010 segment.** The book's `Appendix10_Ibbotson.xlsx` includes Ibbotson's 2004–2010 update (attributed to David Stubbs). We use these values as-is for book replication.
