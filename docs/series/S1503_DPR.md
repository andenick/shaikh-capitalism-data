## 1. Definition

**S1503** is the companion panel (Panel B) to S1502, covering 9 service-sector and government industries: Transportation & Warehousing, Information, Finance/Insurance/Real Estate/Rental/Leasing, Professional & Business Services, Educational/Health/Social, Arts/Entertainment/Accommodation/Food, Other Services (ex govt), Federal Government, State & Local Government. Plus the "All industries" reference benchmark (shared with S1502).

Same formula as S1502: `g_i(t) = (YR_i(t) - YR_i(t-1)) / YR_i(t-1)` (simple percent change, Shaikh's convention verified against the book's "Calculated Growth Rate" columns).

## 2. Why it matters

Together with S1502, S1503 documents the secular shift of US output composition toward services and the divergent growth dynamics between goods-producing sectors (more volatile) and services (smoother trend). Important context for inflation regime analysis in Chapter 15.
