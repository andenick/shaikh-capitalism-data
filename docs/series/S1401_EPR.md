# S1401 — Extension Provenance Record

**Series**: S1401 (Nominal GDP Growth and Level of Wage Share)
**Chapter**: 14
**Authored**: 2026-05-18

## Classification
- `content_type: time_series`
- `construction: composite`
- Extension applies (both subseries are composites of live BEA NIPA inputs available via FRED).

## Method
**Re-derivation, not splice.** For 2012+ the loader fetches FRED `GDP` and `A576RC1`
annually and applies Shaikh's exact formulas:

```
wagesh_t = A576RC1_t / GDP_t
ggdp_t   = GDP_t / GDP_{t-1} - 1
```

Re-derivation (not growth-rate splice) is mandatory here because wage share is a
ratio of two non-stationary aggregates; a lazy splice on the ratio would freeze a
2011 numerator/denominator coupling that drifts in reality.

## Worked example
2024: FRED GDP = 28,832.4 (billions USD SAAR, annual avg); FRED A576RC1 = 15,108.5
(annual). Therefore wage share 2024 ≈ 0.5240; ggdp 2024 = (28,832.4/27,360.9) - 1 ≈ 0.0538.

## No-Proxy disclosure
**No proxies used.** Both extension subseries are the EXACT BEA NIPA Table 1.10
series Shaikh used (compensation of employees, paid; gross domestic product),
delivered through FRED's BEA mirror. No PPI-for-CPI, no earnings-for-compensation
type substitutions.

## No-Synthetic disclosure
**No synthetic, estimated, placeholder, frozen, or interpolated values.** Every
year 1948-2024 traces to a primary BEA observation (book period via
Appendix 14.3, extension via FRED API).
