## 1. Why this series is in principle extendable

S1001 is `time_series`. The underlying BEA Industry Economic Accounts (GDP-by-industry) continue to publish Net Operating Surplus and Investment by industry at annual frequency, so the IROP recipe `ΔNOS / (IG + INV)_{t-1}` can in principle be evaluated through the current year.

## 2. Construction classification

Per the Anu lazy-splice rule: S1001 is a *derived* rate. Any extension must **recompute the formula from extended components** (NOS, IG, INV by industry), not splice on the published rate. This is the No-Lazy-Splices-on-Derived-Quantities rule.

## 3. Why extension is deferred to Phase 6 sensitivity

Per the Phase 4 adequacy report (open question, S1001):

> Shaikh's Appendix 7.2 banking IROP uses a Marx-equivalent *corrected* capital stock that is not directly published by BEA. The published BEA net stocks differ in concept from the book's. Either (a) accept BEA published net stocks with a Concept Match Justification, or (b) re-derive the appendix-7.2 correction. The latter is analytically heavy; the former still requires careful NAICS 5221 vs NAICS 52 disclosure.

Phase 5 therefore produces the book-period S1001 only. Extension is registered as `extension_status: "deferred_to_phase6_sensitivity"` in the loader output. The recommended Phase 6 extension path is documented below for clarity.

## 4. Recommended Phase 6 extension method

```
INPUTS (per industry)
  NOS_industry[t]   = BEA GDP-by-industry, Net Operating Surplus (table 2.7)
  IG_industry[t]    = BEA Investment by Industry, fixed-asset gross investment
  INV_industry[t]   = BEA NIPA Table 5.6.5 (change in private inventories) attributed
                      to industry via current-cost net stocks weighting

INDUSTRY MAPPING (Concept Match Justification)
  Banks (Shaikh 1988-2005) ≈ NAICS 5221 Depository Credit Intermediation (primary)
                            ≈ NAICS 52   Finance & Insurance               (sensitivity, broader)
  All Private (Shaikh)     = Cross-NAICS-private-sector industry mean

FORMULA (same as book)
  IROP_i[t] = (NOS_i[t] - NOS_i[t-1]) / (IG_i[t-1] + INV_i[t-1])

DISCLOSURE
  - BEA published net stocks ≠ Shaikh's appendix-7.2 corrected stock; report
    BEA convention with Concept Match Justification ("Modern BEA net stock
    approximates the book's corrected stock at the industry level; the
    correction primarily affected the all-economy capital aggregate")
  - Flag proxy:true on any extension subseries because the underlying NAICS
    definition shifted from SIC era
```

## 5. Proxies (book period)

## 7. Failure modes & graceful degradation

| Failure | Detection | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Appendix 7.2 workbook missing | Loader checks file existence | Return `status: FAIL, error: file_missing` |
| `Banks` or `All Private` column missing | Loader checks headers | Return `status: FAIL` with column list |
| Future Phase 6 BEA NAICS 5221 unavailable | Loader returns degraded | Publish book period only with `extension_status: api_unavailable` |

## 8. CD2 divergence

CD2's S050 reports spot validation values that diverge from Appendix 7.2. The book's stated primary source is App. 7.2; S1001 reproduces App. 7.2 verbatim. The CD2/App.7.2 divergence is informational only and is logged in the V03 report.
