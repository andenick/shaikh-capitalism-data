# S1502 — Extension Provenance Record

**Series**: S1502 — Growth Rates of Real Output, US Major Industries (Panel A)
**Phase**: 6 (Extension)
**Construction classification**: `formula`
**Extension method**: re-fetch BEA Chain-Type Quantity Index for Gross Output by Industry (modern vintage), recompute `g_i(t) = ln(YR_i(t)) - ln(YR_i(t-1))` for years 2011-present, splice on growth rates (which are scale-invariant under base rebasing)
**Authored**: 2026-05-18
**Author**: opus-subagent-fanout-ch15

---

## 1. Why this series is extendable

S1502 is a `time_series` series of construction `formula`. Per Anu rule, extending a formula series requires re-fetching the formula's components and recomputing — that is what we do here (BEA Industry chain indices remain published; modern vintage 2017=100).

## 2. Method

```
INPUTS
  S1502_book = per-industry growth rates 1988-2010 from chopped table
  BEA_GDP_BY_INDUSTRY (modern vintage) = chain-type quantity indices, levels, 1987-present

EXTENSION
  For each industry i and year t in [2011, current_year]:
    g_i(t) = ln(YR_i(t)) - ln(YR_i(t-1))
  Splice point: 2010 end of book period; 2011+ from BEA modern vintage.
  Because growth rates are invariant under base-year rebasing, no scale factor is required.
```

## 3. No-proxy disclosure

**None.** Same BEA GDP-by-Industry source as the book.

## 4. No-synthetic disclosure

**None.** NaNs propagate. Missing industries (e.g., NAICS reaggregations creating new sector codes) are skipped, not filled.

## 5. Failure-mode table

| Failure | Detection | Action |
|---|---|---|
| `BEA_API_KEY` not set | `S00_config` flags | Loader returns degraded; processor publishes book period only (1988-2010); registry stamped `extension_status: api_key_missing` |
| BEA Industry table schema changed | Loader matches industry labels rather than ordinal positions | Mismatched/dropped industries logged as warnings; never silently substituted |
| NAICS reaggregation | Per-industry growth_rate splice | Documented per-industry in registry; affected industries flagged |

## 6. CD2 divergence pre-disclosure

CD2 used the same BEA Industry source. Pre-2010 growth rates expected to match within rounding; post-2010 may diverge by ~1-3% in some industries due to NAICS reaggregation.
