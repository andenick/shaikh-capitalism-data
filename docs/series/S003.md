# S003: US GDP (MeasuringWorth)

**Chapter 2: Turbulent Trends and Hidden Structures** | **Figures**: Fig2.3 | **Period**: 1780–2024

## What This Measures

U.S. real GDP from MeasuringWorth.com, covering 1790-2010. MeasuringWorth provides historical GDP estimates in constant dollars, constructed from multiple underlying sources including Kuznets, Balke-Gordon, and official BEA NIPA data where available. The series is used as published without additional splicing; it provides the longest continuous GDP series for the United States.
