## 1. Definition

**S1508** is a 29-country **cross-sectional** scatter of country-period averages from Harberger (1988) table 12.11:
- x-axis: `lambda = gDC` — growth rate of Domestic Claims (private + public credit)
- y-axis: `pi` — CPI inflation rate

Both axes in percent (Harberger/Shaikh convention). Shaikh (2016) Figure 15.12 reproduces this scatter as evidence for the cross-country link between credit growth and inflation in high-inflation regimes.

**Phase 4 corrections**: Figure caption says "table 12.1"; correct is **table 12.11** (per narrative + Appendix 15.1 p.897).

## 2. Why it matters

Chapter 15 presents this cross-section as Harberger's empirical anchor for the New Purchasing Power theory of inflation: across countries and across decades, credit growth tracks inflation.
