# S055: Dividend Yield vs Bond Yield

**Chapter 10: Competition, Finance, and Interest Rates** | **Figures**: Fig10.9 | **Period**: 1871–2011

## What This Measures

Compares Shiller S&P dividend yield, 10yr government bond yield, and corporate bond yield from S042. Dividend yield = dividends/price; bond yields are interest rates on government and corporate debt. The comparison tests whether equity and bond yields move together.

## Construction

2. Load S052 for corp bond yield
3. Align
