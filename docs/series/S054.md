# S054: Real Interest Rate HP-filtered

**Chapter 10: Competition, Finance, and Interest Rates** | **Figures**: Fig10.8 | **Period**: 1857–2011

## What This Measures

Real interest rate = nominal bond yield - PPI inflation. HP filter applied with lambda=3 to extract the trend component. The HP filter smooths the series to separate cyclical fluctuations from the underlying trend. Lambda=3 is appropriate for annual data.

## Construction

1. Load S042/S052
2. Compute real rate
3. Apply HP filter lambda=3
