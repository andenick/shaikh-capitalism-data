## 1. Definition

**S1007** plots three closely-related rates of return on Figure 10.7:

- `rreq` — Real equity rate of return = `(Δpreq + dvr) / preq_{t-1}` where `preq = P_Shiller / pIGcorpbea` and `dvr = D_Shiller / pIGcorpbea`
- `iropcorp` — Adjusted corporate real incremental rate of profit (Shaikh's App. 6.8.II.7 `iroprcorp`)
- `iropcorpnipa` — NIPA corporate real incremental rate of profit (Shaikh's `iroprcorpnipa`)

## 2. Why it matters

The empirical hook for Shaikh's claim that the equity rate of return is anchored by the corporate IROP — the central thesis of the entire interest/finance chapter.
