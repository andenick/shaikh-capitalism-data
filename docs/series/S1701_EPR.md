# S1701 — Extension Provenance Record

**Phase**: 6 (Extension)
**Construction**: `composite` (three independent overlay lines)
**Extension method**: re-compute WPI-in-gold from extended WPI + extended gold price; re-apply HP(100)
**Status v1**: `deferred` — book period emitted, post-2007 recomposition deferred to Phase 9

---

## 1. Why this series is extendable (but requires recomposition, not splice)

S1701-A and S1701-B are HP-100-smoothed derived quantities. Per anu-framework
No-Lazy-Splices-on-Derived-Quantities rule, extension MUST recompute the WPI
in gold from extended raw WPI + extended gold price, then re-apply HP(100).
Growth-rate splice on the smoothed series itself is prohibited.

S1701-C (USUKAVGWAVE) is itself derived from S1701-A/B via averaging across
the two completed long waves; its extension is computed analogously.

## 2. Concept Match Justifications (Phase 9 extension)

### US WPI input substitution
- Original: MeasuringWorth "Annual WPI for US 1720-Present" (URL 404 as of
  2026-05-18; dataset no longer published).
- Substitute: NBER macrohistory Warren-Pearson m04051a series (pre-1947)
  spliced to BLS WPU00000000 (post-1947). Same bridge used for Ch5 S502/S504.
- Concept match: both measure the wholesale price level of US tradable goods;
  Warren-Pearson is the pre-1947 standard, WPU00000000 is the BLS successor
  series. Concept-exact (same agency lineage).

### Gold price input
- MeasuringWorth "Price of Gold, 1257-Present"
  (`https://www.measuringworth.com/datasets/gold/`, verified 200 OK).
- Concept-exact.

### UK WPI input
- BoE "A Millennium of Macroeconomic Data" (Sally Hills, Ryland Thomas,
  Nicholas Dimsdale) at `https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/statistics/research-datasets`
  (verified 200 OK).
- Concept match acceptable; overlap-anchor in 1960s-70s window before
  recomposition.

## 3. Method (planned, v1 = deferred)

```
For each country c in {US, UK}:
    WPI[c, year]  = extended WPI series spliced to Shaikh's
    gold[c, year] = MeasuringWorth gold price in c's currency
    WPI_in_gold[c, year] = WPI[c,year] / gold[c, year]
    Re-apply HP(100) over the full extended range
USUKAVGWAVE[year] = mean over the two long-wave intervals projected forward
                    (forecast layer; preserve is_forecast flag)
```

## 4. No-Proxy compliance

US WPI substitution is concept-exact (NBER+BLS bridge). UK WPI substitution
acceptable (BoE Millennium). Both flagged `proxy: false` if Phase 9 uses
identical concept; flagged `proxy: true` only if a different concept is used.

## 5. No-Synthetic compliance

Forecast portion of USUKAVGWAVE is preserved as `is_forecast: True`. Never
imputed; values come directly from Shaikh's projection.

## 6. Failure-mode table

| Failure | Action |
|---|---|
| NBER macrohistory unavailable | Use BLS WPU00000000 from 1947 only |
| BLS WPU unavailable | Mark US extension `api_unavailable` |
| BoE Millennium not refreshed | UK extension capped at last available |

## 7. CD2 divergence pre-disclosure

CD2's S102 was misnamed (IRS table); RSCD reassigned the long-wave figure
to S1701. v1 reproduces the Appendix 5.3 spreadsheet columns exactly.
