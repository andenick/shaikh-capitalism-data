## 1. Definition

**S1506** is a deterministic sub-period view of S1505 covering 1948-1981. Same five subseries (`pi`, `sigma`, `sigma'`, `uL`, `uLintensity`), restricted to the pre-Volcker era. Plots in Shaikh (2016) Chapter 15 split the postwar Phillips relationship into pre/post regimes to illustrate the structural break at the start of the neoliberal era.

## 2. Why it matters

The Volcker disinflation episode (1979-82) marks a regime break in U.S. inflation dynamics. Showing the Phillips relationship separately for the two sub-periods supports Shaikh's argument that the apparent collapse of the conventional Phillips curve post-1980 reflects a regime shift, not the absence of any structural inflation-utilization relationship.
