# S037: US Industry IROP Deviations from Average

**Chapter 7: The Turbulent Equalization of Profit Rates** | **Figures**: Fig7.18 | **Period**: 1988–2005

## What This Measures

Deviations of individual US industry IROPs from the cross-sectional average, 1988-2005. For each industry i and year t: dev_i(t) = IROP_i(t) - IROP_avg(t). Tests the equalization of incremental profit rates across competitive industries.

## Construction

1. Load S215 parsed data or S036 output
2. Compute deviation = industry_IROP - average_IROP per year

## Data Sources
