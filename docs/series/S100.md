## Extension

- **API**: FRED `CMDEBT,PI`
- **Method**: growth_rate splice at 2012
- **Continuity**: Same Flow of Funds household debt series. PI is broader than disposable income but tracks the ratio closely.
- **Source**: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/CMDEBT

Extension via FRED CMDEBT (Household Credit Market Debt Outstanding) / PI (Personal Income). Growth-rate splice of the debt-to-income ratio at 2012. PI used as proxy for disposable personal income.
