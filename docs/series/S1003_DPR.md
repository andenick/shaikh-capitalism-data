## 1. Definition

**S1003** is the *Relative Price of Finance* — the ratio of the long bond yield to the wholesale price index, both rebased to 1947=1. Formula: `rpf_t = (i_t / i_1947) / (p_t / p_1947)`. Shown on Figure 10.3.

## 2. Why it matters

Shaikh argues the *relative* price of finance — interest rate divided by goods price level — is the meaningful long-run measure, because money interest is the price of advancing money in a world where prices themselves drift.

## 4. Construction (derived/formula)

```
S1003[t] = (iblong[t] / iblong[1947]) / (USWPI[t] / USWPI[1947])
```

For extension years, `iblong[t]` becomes FRED AAA[t] and `USWPI[t]` becomes the reindexed PPIACO[t] (per S1002 extension). The 1947 denominators are fixed at the book-period values.

## 5. Year coverage

- Extension: 2012–present (recomputed end-to-end from extended components)
