# S1104 — Extension Provenance Record

**Phase**: 6 (Extension)
**Construction**: `composite` (three independent overlay components)
**Extension method**: per-component (DOTS for trade, BIS PPI EER for REER, OECD QNA + WDI for relative GDP)
**Status v1**: `partial` — book period emitted, extension and S1104-C deferred to Phase 9

---

## 1. Why this series is extendable

Two of three components (trade, REER) inherit the extension paths of S1101-A
and S1102-B respectively. The third (relative real GDP) is a new data demand.
All three are independent overlay lines, so each is extended separately and
re-plotted on the same x-axis.

## 2. Per-component method (planned)

### S1104-A net-trade ratio
Fetch IMF DOTS US X and M, compute `(X-M)/(X+M)`. Same source as S1101-A.

### S1104-B US REER PPI
Inherit S1102-B BIS PPI EER substitute (see `S1102_EPR.md`). Proxy flag
inherited.

### S1104-C US/EU12 relative real GDP
Phase 4 resolution Q5: hold Shaikh's pre-1995 EU12 basket fixed (BE, DK, FR,
DE, GR, IE, IT, LU, NL, PT, ES, GB). Fetch each country's real GDP from
OECD QNA (primary) with WDI NY.GDP.MKTP.KD as backfill, sum across the 12
members in constant USD, divide by US real GDP, rebase 2002=100.

## 3. Concept Match Justifications

- S1104-B BIS substitution: see S1102 EPR §2.
- S1104-C EU12 basket: Concept-exact match (same 12 countries Shaikh used);
  no proxy flag needed.

## 4. No-Proxy compliance

S1104-B inherits proxy=true from S1102. S1104-A and S1104-C are
concept-exact (no proxy).

## 5. No-Synthetic compliance

Any NaN propagates. Belgium pre-1993 in S1104-A is moot (US only).

## 6. Failure-mode table

| Failure | Action |
|---|---|
| IMF DOTS down | S1104-A `extension_status: api_unavailable` |
| BIS down | S1104-B inherits S1102 degradation |
| OECD QNA / WDI missing country | S1104-C basket aggregate NaN for that year |

## 7. CD2 divergence pre-disclosure

CD2's S063 reported trade-balance values matching `(X-M)/(X+M)`; RSCD's
S1104-A reproduces these. S1104-C in CD2 was an analyst-added series with
under-documented EU12 basket; RSCD will produce a fully-documented basket
aggregate in Phase 9.
