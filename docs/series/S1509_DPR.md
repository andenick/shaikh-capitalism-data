## 1. Definition

**S1509** is a 38-country (Romania de-duplicated from 39) **cross-sectional** scatter from Ramamurthy (2014) Chapter 3, extending Harberger's analysis to the 1988-2011 window:
- x-axis: `lambda = gDC` — growth rate of Domestic Claims
- y-axis: `pi = inflation`

Plus a `Inflationary Episode` classification (Acute / Chronic / Control) for each country-period.

Shaikh (2016) Figure 15.13 reproduces this scatter as the modernization of Harberger's empirical finding.

## 2. Why it matters

The Ramamurthy update demonstrates that the credit-inflation relationship Harberger documented for 1970-1988 persists in the post-1988 era, including across the Soviet/Eastern Europe hyperinflation episodes of the early 1990s.
