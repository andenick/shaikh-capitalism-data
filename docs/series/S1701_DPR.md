## 1. Definition

HP-smoothed (lambda=100) US and UK wholesale price indexes expressed in
ounces of gold, plus the average-of-past-two-waves overlay used to project
the 2008-2018 crisis. Appears in Shaikh (2016) as **Figure 17.1** (p. 749);
reuses the data already plotted as Fig 16.1.

Three subseries:
- **S1701-A** US PPI in gold (HP-100) — `USPPIGOLDHP100`, 1800-2007
- **S1701-B** UK PPI in gold (HP-100) — `UKPPIGOLDHP100`, 1801-2007
- **S1701-C** USUKAVGWAVE overlay — 1983-2025 (post-2011 portion = forecast)

## 2. Why it matters in Chapter 17

Fig 17.1 introduces Shaikh's long-wave thesis: 1897-1939 and 1939-1983 are
two completed long waves, each with general crises 8-9 years after the peak.
The dashed average-wave overlay projected the 2008-2018 crisis interval —
a prediction Shaikh first made in 2003.
