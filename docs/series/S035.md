# S035: US Industry Profit Rate Deviations from Average

**Chapter 7: The Turbulent Equalization of Profit Rates** | **Figures**: Fig7.16 | **Period**: 1987–2005

## What This Measures

Deviations of individual US industry profit rates from the cross-sectional average, 1987-2005. For each industry i and year t: dev_i(t) = ROP_i(t) - ROP_avg(t). These deviations test the turbulent equalization hypothesis — they should be stationary, gravitating around zero without systematic trends.

## Construction

1. Load S217 parsed data or S034 output
2. Compute deviation = industry_ROP - average_ROP per year
