# S050: Bank vs Private Industry IROP

**Chapter 10: Competition, Finance, and Interest Rates** | **Figures**: Fig10.1 | **Period**: 1988–2005

## What This Measures

Extracts banking industry IROP from Ch7 S215 data and compares to all-private average. S215 contains industry-level incremental rates of profit; banking industry column is extracted and compared against the cross-sectional average of all private industries.

Chapter 10 examines the relationship between finance, interest rates, and the real economy. Shaikh argues that the interest rate is not determined by supply and demand for loanable funds but by the competitive equalization between financial and industrial rates of return.
