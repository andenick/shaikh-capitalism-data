# S063: US Balance of Trade, Real Exchange Rate, Relative GDP

**Chapter 11: International Competition and the Theory of Exchange Rates** | **Figures**: Fig11.7 | **Period**: 1960–2009

## What This Measures

Load S200 (USJPNdata) Extract US rows (even-indexed per year) S200F -> trade balance, S200B -> REER, S200 -> relative GDP

Chapter 11 develops a classical theory of exchange rates based on real competition. Shaikh shows that real exchange rates are governed by relative real unit labor costs (reflecting international competitiveness) rather than by purchasing power parity or interest rate differentials.
