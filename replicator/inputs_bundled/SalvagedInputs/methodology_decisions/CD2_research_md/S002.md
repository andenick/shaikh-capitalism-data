# S002: US Real Investment

**Chapter 2: Turbulent Trends and Hidden Structures** | **Figures**: Fig2.2 | **Period**: 1832–2025

## What This Measures

Real investment in fixed nonresidential business capital (equipment and structures) constructed from two spliced sources: (1) BEA Fixed Reproducible Tangible Wealth of the United States (1977, table B4, pp. 343-344), Investment in Fixed Nonresidential Business Capital, 1832-1975, originally in 1970=100; (2) BEA Wealth Table 4.8, line 1 (Investment in Fixed Nonresidential Capital), 1901-2010. Both series were rebased to 1958=100 and spliced at 1901 to create a continuous real investment index.

## From the Book

> Investment in Fixed Nonresidential Business Capital, 1832-1975 (1970 = 100) from BEA (1977, table B4) and 1901-2010 from BEA, Wealth Table 4.8, line 1. The two series were rebased to 1958 = 100 and spliced at 1901.
>
> — Shaikh (2016), Appendix 2.1, Figure 2.2

## Data Sources

| Subseries | Source | Period | Units |
|-----------|--------|--------|-------|
| S002-A | BEA Fixed Reprod Tangible Wealth of the US, 1925-75: Table B4, pp. 343-344 | 1832–1975 | Millions of 1958 dollars |
| S002-B | Derived from S002-A | 1832–1975 | Index 1901=100 |
| S002-C | BEA Wealth Table 4.8, line 1, accessed 9/2/2011 | 1901–2010 | Billions of 2005 dollars |
| S002-D | Derived from S002-C | 1901–2010 | Index 1901=100 |
| S002-E | Derived from S002-B+S002-D | 1832–2010 | Index 1901=100 |

## Extension

- **API**: FRED `GPDIC1`
- **Method**: growth_rate splice at 2010
- **Continuity**: BEA real investment continuation in chained 2017 dollars
- **Source**: https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/GPDIC1

Extension via FRED GPDIC1. FRED GPDIC1 (Real Gross Private Domestic Investment, chained 2017 dollars) is the modern continuation of BEA real investment data. GPDIC1 uses chained 2017 dollars vs Shaikh 1970 dollars; growth-rate splice preserves original index levels.
