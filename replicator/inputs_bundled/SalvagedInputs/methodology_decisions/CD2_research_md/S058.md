# S058: Equity Rate vs Corporate ROP

**Chapter 10: Competition, Finance, and Interest Rates** | **Figures**: Fig10.12 | **Period**: 1947–2011

## What This Measures

Corporate equity rate (same construction as Fig10.11) vs Ch6 S026-A average corporate profit rate. The equity return is the real return on equity investment; S026-A is the average rate of profit on corporate capital. Tests whether equity returns gravitate toward the average profit rate.

Chapter 10 examines the relationship between finance, interest rates, and the real economy. Shaikh argues that the interest rate is not determined by supply and demand for loanable funds but by the competitive equalization between financial and industrial rates of return.
