# S068: Nominal GDP Growth and Level of Wage Share

**Chapter 14: The Theory of Wages and Unemployment** | **Figures**: Fig14.10 | **Period**: 1948–2011

## What This Measures

Nominal GDP Growth and Level of Wage Share (Fig14.10) covering 1948-2011. Constructed from Shaikh Appendix 14, ggdp; Shaikh Appendix 14, ggdphp100; Shaikh Appendix 14, wagesh. Extended to the present via FRED GDP,A576RC1.

Chapter 14 develops a classical theory of wages and unemployment. Shaikh shows that the real wage is determined by the balance of power between labor and capital, mediated by the reserve army of labor. The wage share oscillates around a persistent level, with unemployment intensity (not the unemployment rate alone) as the key regulator.
