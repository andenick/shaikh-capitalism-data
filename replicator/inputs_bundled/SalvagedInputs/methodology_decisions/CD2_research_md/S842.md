# S842: Bain 42-Industry Profit Rate vs CR8 (Scatter)

**Chapter 8: Debates on Concentration and Profitability** | **Figures**: Fig8.3 | **Period**: 1936–1940

## What This Measures

Load Bain 42-industry scatter data from L37 Transpose wide industry columns to rows Write cross-sectional output

Chapter 8 critiques the debate between perfect and imperfect competition theories. Shaikh shows empirically that industry concentration (measured by CR4 or CR8 ratios) does not systematically predict profitability — undermining the core claim of the structure-conduct-performance paradigm.

## From the Book

> Figure 8.3 plots the period averages for ROE versus CR8, which he presents in his
table I (309, 312). His central difﬁculty is immediately apparent: there is no evidence
of a positive correlation between concentration and proﬁtability.
>
> — Shaikh (2016), Shaikh (2016), Ch. 8
