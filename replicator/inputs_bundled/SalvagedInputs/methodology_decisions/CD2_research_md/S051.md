# S051: Business Profit Rate and Prime Rate

**Chapter 10: Competition, Finance, and Interest Rates** | **Figures**: Fig10.5 | **Period**: 1947–2011

## What This Measures

Combines prime rate from Federal Reserve (S041 column S041A) with business profit rate from Ch6 S013/S026. The prime rate is the benchmark lending rate; the business profit rate is the average rate of return on capital. Both series are plotted together to show the relationship between interest rates and profit rates.
